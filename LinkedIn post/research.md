# Research: Software as a Commodity — The PropertyHub Narrative

## The Core Thesis

**What you're doing is revolutionary, but quietly so.** You're enabling individuals — property dealers, construction contractors, small business owners — to own their digital destiny for the first time. This isn't about building "websites." It's about giving people **custom digital assets** that were previously reserved for companies with ₹50 lakh+ IT budgets.

---

## The Problem You're Solving

### The Old World (Pre-AI Era)

| Barrier | Reality for Small Business Owner |
|---------|----------------------------------|
| **Cost** | Custom software: ₹15-50 lakhs ($20-60K). Monthly SaaS: ₹25,000-80,000/month |
| **Knowledge Gap** | "I need a developer" — but don't know what to ask for |
| **Dependency** | Locked into 99acres, MagicBricks — paying commissions forever |
| **No Ownership** | Your leads, your data, your brand — all on someone else's platform |
| **One-Size-Fits-All** | Generic tools don't understand Indian property types, local areas, or your workflow |

**The painful irony:** A property dealer earning ₹50 lakhs/year couldn't justify ₹40 lakhs for custom software. So they paid ₹5-10 lakhs/year to platforms that took their leads, their customers, and their margins.

---

## The Shift: Software is Now a Commodity

Three forces have converged:

1. **AI-Assisted Development** — What took 6 months now takes weeks
2. **Cloud Infrastructure Commoditization** — Hosting costs dropped 90% (Vercel, Supabase, etc.)
3. **Open-Source Maturity** — Next.js, Prisma, React — enterprise-grade tools, free

**The result:** The [Democratization of AI market](https://marketresearch.biz/report/democratization-of-ai-market/) is projected to grow from $11.4B (2023) to $119.9B by 2033. [McKinsey reports](https://www.mckinsey.com/industries/technology-media-and-telecommunications/our-insights/how-an-ai-enabled-software-product-development-life-cycle-will-fuel-innovation) that what required "entire engineering teams just two years ago" can now be built by individuals.

---

## What PropertyHub Proves

This codebase is **living evidence** of the shift:

| Feature | SaaS Alternative Cost | PropertyHub Cost |
|---------|----------------------|------------------|
| Lead Management | ₹3,000-25,000/month ([Wise Agent](https://wiseagent.com/): $49, [Follow Up Boss](https://www.followupboss.com/pricing): $69+) | ₹0 (built-in) |
| Property Search with Filters | ₹8,000-15,000/month (Algolia, etc.) | ₹0 (PostgreSQL) |
| Image Gallery + Reordering | ₹5,000-10,000/month (Cloudinary) | ₹0 (Vercel Blob) |
| Services Portfolio | ₹2,000-5,000/month (Showit, Format) | ₹0 (custom built) |
| **Total Monthly** | **₹18,000-55,000** | **₹2,000-5,000** (hosting only) |

**But more importantly — the customization:**
- Indian property types (Plot, Flat, Shop) — not Zillow's "Single Family Home"
- Owner phone tracking — platforms hide this to control the relationship
- Front/back dimensions — specific to Indian plot sales
- Service categories for construction — because he's not just a dealer, he's a builder
